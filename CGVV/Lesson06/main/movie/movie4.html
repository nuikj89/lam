A film about the differences in perspectives among three generations—grandparents, parents, and children. Everyone is right from their own point of view, but when faced with the ambitions of youth, who will ultimately have to listen to the other? And if the dreams of the young are deemed unrealistic, then what opportunities do they have, and when will they finally get to decide their own future?
