In a post-apocalyptic world, a timid gray cat that is scared of water is forced to leave his safe haven when a violent flood destroys its beloved home. On their adventure across the great ocean, the cat and his friends (a capybara, a Labrador dog, a lemur, and a secretary bird) must learn to conquer their anxieties and accept their differences in order to survive.
